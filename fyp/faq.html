FAQ - How to Use the Website

How can I request food?

To request food, please visit our Request Food page and follow the instructions provided.

Then, fill up your credentials

After you have filled up the credentials, the person in charge will contact you via phone or email.

Can I donate food?

Yes, we welcome food donations. Please contact us via email at marketing@uptm.edu.my for donation inquiries.
